Interesting Information about Malawi

  • The Warm Heart of Africa: Malawi tops as a friendly country to most visitors due to the welcoming nature of its people.
  • Lake Malawi: Lake Malawi is one of the biggest and at the same time one of the deepest lakes in Africa.
  • Rich Cultural Heritage: Malawi has a blend of cultures of various ethnic groups which places the country in a position of having various dances, music, festival traditions, etc.
  • Majestic Landscapes: There is the Mulanje Massif, the Nyika Plateau, Zomba Plateau, the Viphya Highlands, the Dedza Wilderness, and much more and ideally is for hiking and wildlife experiences.

How to Stay safe!

  • Malaria Prevention: Use insect repellent and bed nets, and wear long sleeves and pants in the evening.
  • Emergency Numbers: Emergency numbers in Malawi include Police 997 Ambulance 998 and Fire 999.
  • Reliable Transport: Choose well-established taxi service providers and do not take a taxi alone at night. Ensure that you agree on the fare before the car starts to move.
