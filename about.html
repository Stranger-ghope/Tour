About Me

Brief Intro

Hello and welcome to my camping world or the world of adventure. My name is Thomas Kabota and I am a tour guide who has been leading tours for 20 years and love what I do. Due to the passion that I have for Zomba and all of its history, I feel compelled to tell others about the best kept secrets of the area. I invite you to take a virtual guided tour as all activities are thought through in order to provide you with the best experience. It is my pleasure to show you more of Zomba and Malawi!
I am from Malakamu village Traditional Authority Mlumbe in Zomba District. I am proud Father and Husband. I and my Wife have 3 beautiful children (one daughter and two boys).

My Vision

My vision is to provide inspiration to the travelers and establish a link between them and Zomba. I gathered knowledge on how to produce and organize individual and exciting History and Geography learning experiences and would like to share it with you.

My Values

My personal values are honesty, hard work, and professionalism. I truly believe that every traveling should be honest and done with a sense of respect to nature and people.
