My Organization

Name

Nyasaland Charity Foundation

Project Summary

The main objective of the project in the long term is to alleviate the impacts of poverty and make vulnerability a history by introducing a number of interventions. The “Kwakwana Project” will cover a number of activities like supporting orphans and other vulnerable children in terms of health, and education and ensuring that they are free from VAEN issues. It will promote youth development and empower them by giving them technical skills. Support the disabled and end the discrimination that the elderly and the disabled are facing, and not forget to end the chain of poverty and make vulnerability history by teaching the targeted people Financial literacy, Business management, and how to save the little they have by introducing Village savings and loans to the community so that they will be able to be independent and support their families.
